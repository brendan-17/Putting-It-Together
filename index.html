Overview

As the lead architect on my company’s development operations (DevOps) team, I was tasked with creating a strategic document that highlights the key focal points in developing a digital environment. The assignment required me to explore four critical areas: systems design, engineering, and organization; content and information architecture; user experience and visual design; and the impact of misinformation and disinformation in digital spaces. My goal was to identify and prioritize aspects that would be crucial during the project life cycle, ensuring the development of a scalable, efficient, and user-centered system.

To present my findings and final recommendations, I chose to create a website. This platform allows me to showcase my research and reflect on the priorities I would advocate for in the development process. Each section of the website delves into the areas mentioned above, offering insights into the strategies I would emphasize for a successful digital environment. In the conclusion, I reflect on the overall approach, outlining how these priorities align with the project’s objectives and the decisions I would make during the life cycle of the project.
